This will clear all IndexedDB databases to fix the Firestore error.

Manual Steps:

  1. Press F12 to open DevTools
  2. Go to "Application" tab
  3. Expand "Storage" in left sidebar
  4. Click "IndexedDB"
  5. Right-click each database and select "Delete"
  6. Click "Clear site data" at the top
  7. Close ALL tabs with localhost:3001
  8. Open a NEW incognito window
  9. Go to http://localhost:3001
